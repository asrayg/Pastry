What it does

Small app. Big memory.

Everything you copy is kept ready to paste — organized, searchable at a glance, and entirely on your machine.

Pinning

Keep the things you reuse within reach.

Pin an address, a wallet of snippets, the link you paste ten times a day. Pinned items rise to the top and stay there.

  • Pinned items survive “Clear All”
  • They never age out of history
  • One tap, or press P on the selection

Text & images

Screenshots, ready the moment you take them.

Take a screenshot and it's on your clipboard instantly — and in your history like everything else. Oversized Retina captures are scaled to fit instead of being dropped.

  • Text and images captured automatically
  • Matches the ⊞⇧S snipping flow
  • Dimensions and length shown at a glance

On-device

Your clipboard never leaves your Mac.

No account, no sync server, no telemetry. History lives in a single file on your disk. Pastry even respects the content password managers mark as confidential.

  • Nothing is collected or transmitted
  • Concealed & transient items are skipped
  • Native Swift, zero third-party dependencies
